Olympic Games






Project Description:

In this project we explore Olympic games and the data The database we used for this project looks at Olympic records from 1896 to 2016, that were originaly scrapped from www.sports-reference.com. We designed a few queries and identified a few different ways to visualize the data.


Olympic Sports, Countries and thier Medals

























Bill Liu

Description:

This visualization shows the total Olympic medals won by country, year and sports. When you click on the country from the pie chart on the left, you will see their medal trend by year as well as the medal breakdown by sports. For example, US medals are mostly concentrated in athletes and swimming.
